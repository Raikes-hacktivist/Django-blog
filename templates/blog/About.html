ABOUT US

Toretto's blog is a Nigerian Blog created in 2021 from scratch through use of codes.

Toretto's blog is a Nigerin blog that covers topics on tech, gists from universities but mostly in Osun State University(UNIOSUN). We give you the best current news on our page, We are here to give you the right information so get rid of those silly rumors you hear and view our pages for current and real information.

Toretto's blog is a blog website with its headquarter in Osun, Nigeria, focused on delivering latest captivating headlines just the way you love it We are a modern media house with a different view of news gathering and delivery. We are aware of the fake news trend in the online media and have taken up the challenge to correct this trend. We are out to rewrite the rules on how news publishing should be practiced in a digital age.

The team at toretto's blog delivers reliable and verified Nigerian, Africa and world headlines in a simple, easy-to-read format; just the way news should be delivered, with a dedicated team of editors. All contents on Toretto's are verified and published on the sound judgement of our seasoned editors.

Toretto's blog is of the view that the media can positively or negatively influence people's individual and collective decision making processes, with a massive multiplier effect on the society, hence, we have chosen to help build our society through accurate reportage.
